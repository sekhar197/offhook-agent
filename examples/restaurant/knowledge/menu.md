# Menu

## Bruschetta
Grilled bread, marinated tomatoes, basil, olive oil. Vegetarian.

## Burrata
Creamy burrata, roasted peppers, balsamic. Vegetarian, gluten-free.

## Potato Gnocchi
House gnocchi in sage butter or pomodoro. Vegetarian.

## Spaghetti Carbonara
Guanciale, egg, pecorino, black pepper.

## Eggplant Parmigiana
Layered eggplant, marinara, mozzarella. Vegetarian.

## Branzino
Whole roasted Mediterranean sea bass, lemon, herbs. Gluten-free.

## Chicken Marsala
Pan-seared chicken, marsala mushroom sauce.

## Tiramisu
Classic espresso-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone. Vegetarian.

## Gluten-Free Options
Burrata, branzino, and most secondi can be prepared gluten-free. Tell us about allergies and we'll have a manager confirm.
