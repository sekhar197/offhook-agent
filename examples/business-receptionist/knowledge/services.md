# Services

## Teeth Cleaning
Routine cleaning and check-up. About 45 minutes. Covered by most insurance plans.

## Whitening
In-office whitening session, about an hour. Not usually covered by insurance.

## Dental Crown
Two visits: prep and fitting. Ask about same-day crowns on Wednesdays.

## Emergency Visit
Same-day slots held every morning for urgent pain or a broken tooth. Call as early as you can.
