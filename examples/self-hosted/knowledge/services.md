# Services

## Acupuncture
60-minute sessions with a licensed acupuncturist. First visit includes a consultation.

## Massage Therapy
30, 60, or 90-minute therapeutic massage. Deep tissue and relaxation styles available.

## Wellness Consultation
A 45-minute intake to plan a care program. Bring any recent lab results.

## New Patient Paperwork
Arrive 15 minutes early for your first visit, or ask us to email the forms ahead of time.
