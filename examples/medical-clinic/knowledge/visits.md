# Visits

## Annual Physical
Comprehensive yearly check-up, about 30 minutes. Most plans cover it fully.

## Sick Visit
Same-day or next-day slots for new, non-emergency illness.

## Vaccinations
Flu, COVID, Tdap, and routine childhood and adult vaccines.

## Lab Work
On-site blood draws; fasting labs are best booked in the morning.

## Wellness & Physicals for School/Sports
Forms completed during the visit — bring any paperwork.

## Prescription Refills
Refill requests are taken as a message for the nurse and handled within one business day. We cannot advise on medications by phone.
